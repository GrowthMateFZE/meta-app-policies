Data Deletion Instructions

Growth Mate Agents App — Last updated: April 1, 2026

How to Request Data Deletion

Growth Mate Agents is an internal business tool used by GrowthMate FZE to manage advertising campaigns on behalf of our clients. If you would like to request deletion of any data associated with your account or ad data accessed through this application, follow the steps below.

Send a deletion request to:
team@growth-mate.com

Include "Data Deletion Request" in the subject line, along with your name and any relevant account identifiers.

What Happens Next

  1. Acknowledgment — We will confirm receipt of your request within 2 business days.
  2. Verification — We may contact you to verify your identity and confirm the scope of deletion.
  3. Deletion — All data associated with your request will be permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days.
  4. Confirmation — You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete.

What Data Can Be Deleted

  • Ad account performance data stored locally by our systems
  • Reports and analysis generated from your ad account data
  • Custom audience data created or managed through our platform

Limitations

We cannot delete data that is stored directly by Meta on their platform. To manage data held by Meta, please visit your Facebook Settings.
