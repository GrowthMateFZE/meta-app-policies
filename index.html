Privacy Policy

Growth Mate Agents App — Last updated: April 1, 2026

1. About This App

Growth Mate Agents ("the App") is an internal business tool operated by GrowthMate FZE ("Growth Mate", "we", "us"). The App connects to the Meta Marketing API to manage advertising campaigns and measure performance on behalf of our agency clients.

This App is not available to the general public. It is used exclusively by Growth Mate team members to service client advertising accounts under signed service agreements.

2. Data We Access

Through the Meta Marketing API, the App accesses:

  • Ad account performance data (impressions, clicks, conversions, spend, ROAS)
  • Campaign, ad set, and ad-level configuration and metrics
  • Audience insights and custom audience management
  • Creative asset performance data

We do not access personal user data, Facebook profiles, friend lists, or private messages.

3. How We Use the Data

Data accessed through the App is used solely to:

  • Monitor and optimize advertising campaigns for our clients
  • Generate performance reports
  • Identify winning and underperforming ad creatives
  • Make data-informed budget and targeting decisions

4. Data Storage and Security

API credentials are stored in environment variables on secured local systems. Performance data pulled from the API is stored locally and in secured cloud storage accessible only to authorized Growth Mate team members. We do not sell, share, or distribute Meta API data to any third parties.

5. Data Retention

We retain ad performance data for the duration of our service agreement with each client. Upon termination of a client relationship, data is deleted within 90 days unless otherwise required by law.

6. Your Rights

If you are a client or data subject and wish to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data, please contact us at team@growth-mate.com.
